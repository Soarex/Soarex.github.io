Seidon Editor

Every good game engine needs a good editor

About

Seidon Editor is a simple and intuitive tool to create games with Seidon Game Engine. It does everything you'd expect a modern editor to do, all without taking ages to load, usign half of your storage and requiring a high end pc. Who would have tought!

Transformation Guizmos

What kind of editor would it be without guizmos? Quickly position, scale and rotate your entities with ease

Drag and Drop Everything!

The most intuitive way to setup your entities. Need to change a material? Just drag and drop it

Flexible Layout

Reposition and scale all the windows to your likings, all with docking support

Completly customizable systems

The editor is made exactly like every other Seidon application, so no special untouchable systems. Don't like the default Render System? Just replace it

Prefab support

Save a preconfigured entity with all of its components and quickly replicate it

Hot load extensions

Just load your extensions and all of your custom components and systems will magically appear
