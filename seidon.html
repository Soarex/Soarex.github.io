Seidon

A simple and fast game engine

About

Seidon is a simple and fast game engine written in c++ and opengl. Development started in August 2021 and is now feature complete, meaning it can now be used to create beautiful 3d games.

Full PBR Workflow

Make beautiful games with the power of physically based rendering

Based on the ECS pattern

Handle thousands of entities without breaking a sweat, even on less powerful hardware

Physx powered physics engine

Effortless realistic physics simulation

Native c++ extensions

Similar to scripts, without the overhead of a vm. Just register your components and systems and the reflection system will take care of everything

Built in character controller

One of the most important parts of a game, ready to go
